Biography

Ken Moselle is the director of the Applied Clinical Research Unit (ACRU) at Vancouver Island Health Authority (Island Health). He is an adjunct associate professor of clinical psychogy at University of Victoria, research affiliate of the Institute on Aging and Lifelong Health (IALH), and a co-founder of Data Science Studio.
